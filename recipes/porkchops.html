Pan-Fried Pork Chops

Description

Few things are as easy to prepare as these pan-fried boneless pork chops. They take only minutes to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless pork chops, 3/4-inch thick
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil

Steps

  1. Season both sides of chops with salt and pepper, sprinkle with flour. Sprinkle evenly over entire surface.
  2. Heat oil in 12 inch skillet over medium heat. Add chops and fry until brown, 4 minutes.
  3. When edges brown, flip, cook until themometer reads 145
