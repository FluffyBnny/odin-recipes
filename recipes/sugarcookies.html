Sugar Cookies

Description

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this top-rated sugar cookie recipe. It comes together with just a few ingredients you likely already have on hand â€” and the sugar cookies turn out perfect every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Stir flour, baking soda, baking powder together in a small bowl
  3. Beat sugar and butter together until smooth. Beat in egg and vanilla Gradually blend in flour mixture.
  4. Roll dough into walnut sized balls, place 2 inches apart on ungreased sheet
  5. Bake in oven until golden, 8-10 minutes. Cool completely.
