Easy Meatloaf

Description

Get back to basics with this easy meatloaf recipe. It's quick, traditional, delicious, and it's sure to please everyone at your table.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease 9x5 loaf pan
  2. Combine beef, onion, milk, panko and egg in large bowl; season with salt and pepper. Transfer to pan.
  3. Mix ketchup,brown sugar, and mustard in a small bowl. Evenly spread over top of meatloaf.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until no longer pink. About 1 hour.
